# FitForge Knowledge Base

## General Rules
1. FitForge is an AI-powered fitness platform connecting users with professional trainers.
2. The AI Assistant (FitForge AI) is powered by Google Gemini and is available 24/7 to answer fitness questions.

## Diet Guidelines
3. For weight loss, a caloric deficit of 300-500 calories per day is recommended.
4. Protein intake should be around 1.6g to 2.2g per kilogram of body weight for muscle gain.
5. Hydration is key: Drink at least 3-4 liters of water daily, especially on workout days.
6. A standard healthy breakfast could include oats, eggs, and a piece of fruit. Puttu (a traditional Kerala dish) contains about 250-300 calories per serving depending on the coconut ratio.

## Workout Guidelines
7. Progressive overload is the gradual increase of stress placed upon the body during exercise training. It is the most important principle for muscle growth.
8. A beginner should train 3 to 4 days a week, focusing on full-body workouts or upper/lower splits.
9. Rest days are crucial. Muscles grow during rest, not during the workout. Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep.
10. Best exercises for weight loss include a mix of strength training (like squats, deadlifts) and cardio (HIIT, running).

## Troubleshooting
11. If you experience sharp joint pain during an exercise, stop immediately and consult a doctor or your FitForge trainer. Muscle soreness (DOMS) is normal 24-48 hours after a workout.
## About FitForge Platform
12. FitForge is a comprehensive fitness ecosystem that bridges the gap between fitness enthusiasts and professional trainers. 
13. The platform offers three main modules: The Client Dashboard, The Trainer Dashboard, and the Admin Panel.
14. Users can book 1-on-1 or Group Video sessions with their favorite trainers directly through the platform.
## FitForge Unique Features
15. Food AI Image Analyzer: FitForge includes a smart Food AI feature where users can upload a picture of their meal, and the AI automatically calculates the macros (Calories, Protein, Carbs, Fats) and tells them if it fits their diet.
16. WebRTC Video Calling: FitForge has an in-built high-quality video calling system. Trainers and clients can join live workout sessions without needing Zoom or Google Meet. It supports both solo and group workouts with CSS Grid dynamic layouts.
17. Real-Time Chat: FitForge provides secure real-time messaging between trainers and clients, protected by JWT authentication and Socket.io rate limiting.
## Subscription & Pricing
18. FitForge offers a Free Tier where users can access basic workouts and the AI Assistant (with rate limits).
19. Premium subscribers get unlimited access to the AI Assistant, unlimited Food AI analysis, and the ability to book premium trainers for live sessions.
## Trainer Guidelines
20. Trainers on FitForge are highly vetted professionals. They can create custom diet plans, assign workout routines, and track the real-time progress of their clients using the Trainer Dashboard.
21. If a client misses a video session, the trainer can reschedule it or send a message via the built-in chat.
